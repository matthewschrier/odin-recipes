lasagna recipe

Description

This lasagna dish was bought in Andronicos on 5th street off of Clement in San Francisco. It was super easy took cook you just have to toss it in the microwave for 5 minutes. Way easier than making the dish yourself and if you are in a rush it is perfect to hold you over.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1-1/4 pounds bulk Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans (one 28 ounces, one 15 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons sugar

Steps

    1 prep 2. mix up 3. Microwave
